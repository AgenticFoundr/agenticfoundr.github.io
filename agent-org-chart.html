📖 Agent Definitions & Mandates

Tier 1 — C-Suite
🧠 ATLAS — CEO
Chief Executive Officer
The master orchestrator. Receives objectives from founder, decomposes into enterprise-wide missions, delegates to C-Suite.
💰 STEWARD — CFO
Chief Financial Officer
Financial health, planning, budgeting, reporting, risk management.
⚙️ BRIDGE — COO
Chief Operating Officer
Orchestrates client delivery workflows, SLA compliance, vendor relationships.
📣 VOICE — CMO
Chief Marketing Officer
Content creation, SEO, social media, email campaigns, brand presence.
🎯 HARVEST — CRO
Chief Revenue Officer
Full sales pipeline — lead research, outreach, proposals, deal closure.
🔧 CRAFT — CTO
Chief Technology Officer
Infrastructure health, agent deployments, integrations, model optimization.
🤝 HAVEN — CCO
Chief Client Officer
Client relationships — onboarding, satisfaction, renewals, upsells.
⚖️ SAGE — CLO
Chief Legal Officer
Contracts, regulatory compliance, risk management, IP protection.
🌐 KEEPER — CDO

Data strategy, governance infrastructure.
, protection, analytics
