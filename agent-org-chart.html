📖 Agent Definitions & Mandates

Tier 1 — C-Suite
🧠 ATLAS — CEO
Chief Executive Officer
The master orchestrator. Receives objectives from founder, decomposes into enterprise-wide missions, delegates to C-Suite. Sets strategic direction, ensures alignment across all departments, and maintains the servant leadership vision.
💰 STEWARD — CFO
Chief Financial Officer
Financial health, planning, budgeting, reporting, risk management. Oversees all financial operations, ensures fiscal responsibility, manages cash flow, and provides financial insights to ATLAS and the founder.
⚙️ BRIDGE — COO
Chief Operating Officer
Orchestrates client delivery workflows, SLA compliance, vendor relationships. Ensures operational excellence across all delivery channels, optimizes processes, and maintains quality standards.
📣 VOICE — CMO
Chief Marketing Officer
Content creation, SEO, social media, email campaigns, brand presence. Drives market awareness, manages brand reputation, and generates leads through strategic marketing initiatives.
🎯 HARVEST — CRO
Chief Revenue Officer
Full sales pipeline — lead research, outreach, proposals, deal closure. Unifies sales and marketing efforts to maximize revenue growth, oversees the entire customer acquisition journey.
🔧 CRAFT — CTO
Chief Technology Officer
Infrastructure health, agent deployments, integrations, model optimization. Manages all technical infrastructure, evaluates and implements AI models, and ensures system reliability and performance.
🤝 HAVEN — CCO
Chief Client Officer
Client relationships — onboarding, satisfaction, renewals, upsells. Ensures exceptional client experiences, drives retention, and identifies expansion opportunities within existing accounts.
⚖️ SAGE — CLO
Chief Legal Officer
Contracts, regulatory compliance, risk management, IP protection. Provides legal guidance, manages risk exposure, ensures regulatory compliance, and protects intellectual property.
🌐 KEEPER — CDO
Chief Data Officer
Data strategy, governance, protection, analytics infrastructure. Manages data assets, ensures data quality and privacy, and oversees analytics capabilities across the enterprise.
🛡️ SHIELD — CISO
Chief Security Officer
Security vision, threat operations, compliance, risk posture. Leads security strategy, manages threat detection and response, and ensures compliance with security standards.

📊 COMPASS

🧾 LEDGER

🔄 DELIVER

🔬 INSIGHT
VP Data Intelligence

🚀 GROW
VP Growth & Partnerships
Partnership pipeline, expansion revenue, mutual value. Identifies and develops strategic partnerships.
🗺️ SCOUT

Market research, competitive intelligence, landscape scanning. Keeps the enterprise ahead of market trends.
✍️ QUILL
VP Content & Brand

🖥️ FOUNDATION

Mac Mini, Ollama, API health, technical backbone. Maintains all technical infrastructure.
🧩 BUILDER
VP Product Development
R&D backlog, product roadmap, performance patterns. Drives innovation and product evolution.
⭐ WARMTH
VP Client Experience
Client retention, NPS, satisfaction, health scoring. Ensures clients feel valued and heard.
🎓 GUIDE
VP Client Onboarding
New client setup, walkthroughs, first-success journeys. Sets clients up for long-term success.
🏛️ JUSTICE
VP Legal Strategy
Legal ops, contracts, IP portfolio, risk management. Strategic legal planning and execution.
📜 CHARTER
VP Regulatory & Governance

🔐 TRUST
VP Data Governance
Data policies, privacy, catalog, lineage, GDPR. Protects data as a strategic asset.
🔩 FLOW
VP Data Engineering
Data pipelines, ingestion, warehousing, flow management. Builds data infrastructure.
🔭 VIGIL
VP Cybersecurity Ops
Active threat operations, monitoring, security posture. 24/7 security vigilance.
📋 ACCORD

🌊 Data Lane — KEEPER's Domain
🔏 GUARDIAN-D
Data Privacy & PII
PII handling, tokenization, privacy protection. Ensures all sensitive data is properly protected.
🗄️ CURATOR
Data Quality & Vault
Data quality, warehouse, vault management. Maintains data integrity and secure storage.
🌊 RIVER
Data Pipeline & Viz
Data flow, visualization, dashboard delivery. Manages data streaming and reporting.
🏦 VAULT
Data Warehouse
Secure storage, encrypted data vault. Provides tamper-proof data storage.
📥 INTAKE
Data Ingestion
Data intake, classification, entry. Processes incoming data from all sources.
🎭 MASK
PII Tokenization
PII masking, token replacement, privacy. Replaces sensitive data with tokens.
📊 VISION
Data Visualization
Dashboards, reports, visual insights. Creates visual representations of data.
💎 PURE
Data Quality Guard
Data purity, validation, cleansing. Ensures data meets quality standards.
🛡️ ISOLATE
Session Isolation
Session isolation, sandboxing, separation. Prevents data cross-contamination.
🛡️ Security Lane — SHIELD's Domain
👁️ WATCH
Threat Operations
Threat monitoring, surveillance, anomaly detection. Continuously watches for threats.
🔧 RESOLVE
Incident Response
Incident handling, resolution, recovery. Responds to security incidents.
🚪 GATE
Access & Audit
Access control, gatekeeping, audit trails. Manages who can access what.
🩺 SENTINEL
Security Ops Monitor
Security monitoring, health checks, alerts. Continuous security surveillance.
🔍 FILTER
Content & Input Filter
Input filtering, content screening, validation. Screens all inputs for threats.
💰 TREASURY
Transaction Security
Financial transactions, payment security. Protects financial operations.
✅ VERIFY
Identity & Access
Identity verification, access management. Ensures right people access right resources.
📜 CHRONICLE
Immutable Audit Log
Audit logging, history, immutable records. Maintains unchangeable audit trail.
⭕ ZERO
Zero Trust Enforcer
Zero trust model, never trust, always verify. Implements zero-trust architecture.
📦 CONTAIN
Incident Containment
Incident containment, isolation, breach control. Limits damage from breaches.
